At SAKALE FASHIONS N'CREATIONS., we believe that a bag is not just a practical accessory, but a reflection of one's personal style and values.

That's why we're committed to creating high-quality bags that are not only functional, but also beautiful and sustainable.

  • In addition to our commitment to sustainability, we are also committed to ethical manufacturing practices.
  • Our bags are made in a factory that offers fair wages and safe working conditions to its workers, and we visit the factory regularly to ensure that our standards are upheld.
  • At, we offer a range of bags to meet the needs of our diverse customer base.

From backpacks and totes to duffle bags and messenger bags, our products are designed to be durable, functional and stylish. We use only the highest quality materials, such as recycled nylon and organic cotton, and pay close attention to every detail, from the stitching to the hardware.
